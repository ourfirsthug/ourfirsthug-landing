About Us

Divyansh Nigam
Co-founder & CEO
Aman Pandey
Co-founder & CTO

Weddings are once-in-a-lifetime — and your memories deserve to be captured beautifully, effortlessly, and honestly. Yet, finding the right photographer often feels frustrating: inflated prices, fake reviews, and portfolios that don’t match expectations. On the other side, talented photographers struggle to find genuine clients who value their craft.

ourfirsthug was born from this gap.

We’re building a curated platform where love meets trust — a seamless space for couples to discover professional photographers who fit their style and budget, and for photographers to showcase their artistry, build packages, and secure meaningful work.

Behind this is Divyansh Nigam, who spent 2+ years working alongside wedding photographers and saw the pain points up close. Alongside him is Aman Pandey, who has a deep passion for clean, user-first experiences and a strong background in building reliable digital products that scale.

Together, we’re on a mission to bring clarity, elegance and ease to wedding memories.

Because your first hug as a married couple deserves to be captured by someone who truly sees it.
